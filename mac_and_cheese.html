Mac and Cheese

An American classic! Ooey-gooey Mac and cheese!

It's creamy, it's cheesy, it's a dream! I don't know about you, but the people I've met that don't like mac and cheese are few and far between. Enjoy this quick and easy comfort food tonight!

Ingredients

  • Pasta of your choosing
  • Salted butter
  • Shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • Milk

Steps

  1. In a small pot, boil some pasta, and while that cooks, in a separate pot, stir together a little butter and flour. Whisk in milk until the sauce thickens and then add cheese.
