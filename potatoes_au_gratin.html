Potatoes Au Gratin

Easy and Cheesy Potatoes Au Gratin!

Cheese. Potatoes. Yummy goodness. Get in mah belly!

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Cheddar
  • Gruyere
  • Milk

Steps

    1. Prepare potatoes and onions by thinly slicing. For even thin slices, I use an inexpensive mandoline. You can find them for under $20 it and saves a lot of time.

    2. Make the cheese sauce per the recipe below with flour and butter. Add in a little bit of milk to make a white sauce and finally lots of cheese. If desired other additions such as garlic and fresh thyme or herbs can be added.

    3. Assemble the gratin. Stack the potato slices and onions in little piles and then stand them on their sides in your casserole dish (instead of laying them flat like a traditional gratin). This is easy to do and makes this potatoes au gratin recipe really pretty!
